## Sioux Falls, SD

### Places to Eat
- MacKenzie River Pizza (Rancher - a very meaty pizza - delicious)
- Tokyo (best Sushi and Rolls in town)
- Johnny Carino's
- Nickelspot

### Things to Do
- Falls Park
- Empire Mall
- Pallisades
- Downtown (shopping, Museums, Big Sioux River walk, skulpture walk - very cool)
